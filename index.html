About Us

DTD Services Limited is a Company duly incorporated in Nigeria to provide logistic and support services to foreign investors, new entrants, and existing companies in the Nigerian market. The Company, which has its headquarters in Lagos, is well equipped to cater for all the logistic requirements of our clients in Nigeria, thereby providing them invaluable service that allows them focus on their core activities. This enhances their productivity and enables them cut costs.

The company has a Management Team that has over a decade of industry experience, coupled with seasoned professionals who provide administrative, financial, technical and logistics support for the effective and efficient performance of our clients operations. Our services are time definite and customized to meet the individual and specialized needs of our clients.
