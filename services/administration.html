Administration

Our service is total in concept as it takes care of your needs from your entry into the country until you are ready to depart, or have settled in. Our administration services include:

  • Airport and Protocol services which include “receive and dispatch” assistance, ticketing arrangement, check-in assistance, immigration clearance etc.
    • procurement of new Expatriate Quotas as well as renewal of expiring Expatriate Quotas.
    • procurement of visitors/business visas.
    • renewal of entry/visitors/business visas.
    • regularization formalities which include Resident permit, multiple re-entry visas, documentation etc. where applicable.
  • Temporary accommodation — fully furnished and serviced luxury apartments in Ikoyi and Apapa areas of Lagos.
  • Local transportation services; chauffeur driven vehicles with security personnel, including “water taxi” for our clients (in Lagos),
  • Fully automated temporary office space with internet services, telephone, fax to enable our clients conduct their business efficiently.
  • Procurement of armed security services.
  • Recruitment of both skilled and unskilled personnel.
  • IT consultancy.
  • Modern day beach house, with 16-seat speedboat available for recreation.
